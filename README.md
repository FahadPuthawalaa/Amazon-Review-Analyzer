Amazon Review & Complaint Analyzer Pro is a free Chrome extension that helps Amazon sellers instantly understand what customers love and hate about their products — powered by Google Gemini AI.
What it does:

🔍 Sentiment Analysis — breaks down positive, neutral & negative reviews with visual bars
⚠️ Complaint Intelligence — extracts the top recurring issues with severity ratings
💡 Seller Action Plan — gives 5 concrete steps to improve your product & rating
✍️ AI Reply Generator — writes professional responses to negative reviews in seconds
📊 Keyword & Competitive Analysis — finds what words customers use and which competitors they mention

Works on amazon.com, amazon.in, amazon.co.uk and 4 other Amazon marketplaces.
100% free — no subscription, no ads. Just add your free Google Gemini API key and start analyzing in seconds.
